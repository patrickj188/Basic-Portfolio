ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Patrick Johnson. I am from Las Vegas, NV but have lived all over the states. I am a free spirit that has a passion for travel and film. I have been all over the world but still, have much to see. I ended up in Dallas for a job but it was a great move! My girlfriend eventually joined me down in Dallas. We adopted a dog named Levon, he is a boarder collir Shepard mix. I am a huge music fan with a love for experimental music, metal and hip-hop.
